Explore the Farm

Our farm offers a range of fun and relaxing activities, take a peaceful stroll along the scenic nature trails!

Petting Zoo

Get up close with our friendly farm animals! Our petting zoo is a hit with families and kids. You’ll meet goats, rabbits, sheep, and more!

Scenic Walks and Nature Trails

Take a peaceful walk through our beautiful countryside. We offer guided nature walks where you can enjoy stunning views and local wildlife.

Play Park for Children

Let the kids run wild in our safe and fun play park. With slides, swings, and climbing frames, it’s the perfect place for them to burn off some energy!

Café Serving Homemade Cakes and Hot Drinks

Relax in our cozy café with a selection of homemade cakes, fresh coffee, and hot drinks. Perfect after a walk or while enjoying the view!

Our Products

Our farm produces Raw Milk, Fresh Vegtables, Fruits, and different variety of plants, and some souvenir's!

Who We Are

Located between Edinburgh and Carlisle, our farm offers a relaxing escape with activities for all ages. Whether you’re looking to shop for fresh, locally-grown produce, enjoy a day out with your family, or take a scenic walk through the countryside, our farm has something for everyone!
